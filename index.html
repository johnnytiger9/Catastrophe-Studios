Tribes is a real-time strategy game where survival means outsmarting, outgrowing, and out-fighting every other tribe on the island. Do you have what it takes to build the strongest tribe and conquer the land or will you be wiped out by stronger tribes and foes?

Start from humble beginnings with just a small group of villagers and expand your tribe by gathering resources, constructing buildings, and training warriors. Every decision you make will shape the future of your people. Will you focus on rapid expansion? Fortifying your defenses? Or launch aggressive attacks to dominate early on. Master your own playstyle and be the greatest there is in Tribes!

Whether you play against AI tribes, challenge real players in online battles or team up with friends in multiplayer co-op. Every match offers a unique and dynamic experience, adapt to changing battlefields, form alliances (or betray them), and prove your strategic skill!



Features:

Build and Conquer: Expand your village, gather resources and raise an unstoppable army.

Real-time Battles: Engage in fast-paced skirmishes where every unit and attack matters

Single-player and multiplayer: fight against AI in a challenging campaign or compete with, and against other players online.

Dynamic world: Changing terrain weather and events make every game unpredictable.

Tribal warfare: Unlock and research unique units and abilities to customize your playstyle and lead your people to victory

ABOUT US

Catastrophe studios: founded in November 2024, based in Ireland/The Netherlands.
Founded by two young teens from across the seas, brought together by the drive to create the greatest and most memorable content. Arda and Dillion met each other in late 2021 and were always driven to create. Their first game working together, "Willys Wonderful Wonders", sparked a light of creativity and a life time of ideas. Arda and Dillion started tribes in November 2024 and since then made Catastrophe Studios and expanded he team.
